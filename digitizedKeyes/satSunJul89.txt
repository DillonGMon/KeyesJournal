Sat and Sun July 8 and 9
6 A.M. Took hack to depot and St Louis House. Breakfast at St Louis House – 10 A.M. went up incline railway to Deluth heights – Deserted [Tom] – Bought N.P.R.R. tickets. Took car to Saleside park – Deep gorge and falls – Went down on lakeshore Shippee, Sayles and Chittenden were with me – Armed ourselves throwing items with into the lake at 3 P.M. to back to Duluth at 3 P.M. Bought sweater and knife. Wrote letters to mother and father. Took dinner at Spalding House 7.30 Boarded train for Staples.
